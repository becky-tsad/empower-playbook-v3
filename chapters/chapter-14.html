Training Modules

Available through the Empower Academy. No one touches a family's case until they've completed the required training.

Program Managers 4 min read

Available through the Empower Academy. No one touches a family's case until they've completed the required training.

Core Training Sequence

Training is not onboarding. It's methodology certification. Every staff member and partner must complete the relevant modules before engaging with participants.

Module Duration Audience Required? What It Covers
Empower Methodology Overview 2 hours All staff Yes The four Non-Negotiables, three-team architecture, poverty exit vs. poverty management, the evidence base
Benefits Cliff Navigation 4 hours Navigators, Lead Navigator Yes Cliff identification, calculator tools, transition sequencing, employer coordination during cliff crossings
Navigator Best Practices 6 hours Navigators Yes Relationship-based coordination, wraparound protocols, caseload management, problem-solving frameworks, when to escalate
Employer Certification Training 2 hours Employer Liaison Yes Certification requirements, Poverty IQ curriculum, wage subsidy administration, retention partnership protocols
Poverty IQ (for Employers) 1 hour Certified Employers Yes ALICE realities, benefits cliff effects on employees, why workers decline promotions, retention strategies
ALICE Orientation 30 min All partners Recommended Who ALICE families are, what they face, how to interact without assumptions
Data Dashboard Training 2 hours Project Coordinator, Navigators Yes System access, data entry protocols, report generation, real-time tracking, privacy/security requirements
Leadership Alignment Workshop 4 hours Leadership Team Recommended Shared vision development, decision frameworks, vision drift prevention, three-team coordination
Grant Writing for Empower 3 hours Grant Staff Recommended Funder segmentation, pipeline math, pitch framing by audience, common mistakes
SmartDollar / Financial Coaching 2 hours Navigators Yes (if using SmartDollar) Platform navigation, Baby Steps framework, engagement strategies, prosperity journal integration

Ongoing Training Calendar

Initial certification is the beginning, not the end.

Frequency Training Activity Who
Monthly Navigator peer learning — case conferences, barrier solution sharing All navigators
Quarterly Return to shared vision — methodology adherence check All staff
Quarterly Benefits cliff policy updates — recalibrate for threshold changes Navigators, Lead Navigator
Semi-annually Employer engagement refresher Employer Liaison, Program Director
Annually Full methodology recertification All staff
As needed New hire onboarding — full training sequence, not "shadow someone" New staff
